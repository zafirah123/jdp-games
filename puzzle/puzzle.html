Game Rules

1.

Goal: Swap scrambled block pieces to complete the cartoon reference picture.

2.

How to Play: Tap any piece on the grid to select it, then tap another unlocked piece to swap their positions.

3.

Auto-Locking: Once a piece is aligned into its correct position, it will automatically lock in place, glow with a golden border, and cannot be swapped again.

4.

Scoring: Earn points for every single block piece you correctly lock into its correct position!

5.

Time Limit: You have exactly 3 minutes (180s). Beat the timer and see how high you can score!
